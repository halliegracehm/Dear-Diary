# 🕯️ Dear Diary — Your Journaling App

## How to put this live on the internet (step by step)

Don't worry — this is easier than it looks. Just follow these steps one at a time.

---

### STEP 1 — Create a free GitHub account
1. Go to **github.com**
2. Click "Sign up" and create a free account
3. Verify your email

---

### STEP 2 — Create a new GitHub repository
1. Once logged in, click the **+** button in the top right corner
2. Click **"New repository"**
3. Name it: `dear-diary`
4. Leave everything else as default
5. Click **"Create repository"**

---

### STEP 3 — Upload your files to GitHub
1. On your new repository page, click **"uploading an existing file"**
2. Drag ALL the files from this folder into the upload area
   - Make sure to include the `src` folder and everything inside it!
3. Click **"Commit changes"**

---

### STEP 4 — Create a free Vercel account
1. Go to **vercel.com**
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Choose **"Continue with GitHub"** — this connects the two accounts

---

### STEP 5 — Deploy your app
1. On Vercel, click **"Add New Project"**
2. Find your `dear-diary` repository and click **"Import"**
3. Leave all settings as default
4. Click **"Deploy"**
5. Wait about 60 seconds ⏳
6. Vercel will give you a live URL like: `dear-diary.vercel.app` 🎉

---

### STEP 6 — Add it to your halliewho.com website
1. Copy your new Vercel URL
2. Log into your WordPress dashboard
3. Go to **Appearance → Menus**
4. Add a new "Custom Link" to your menu
   - URL: your Vercel link
   - Label: "✨ Dear Diary Journal"
5. Save your menu

---

### That's it! Your app is live. 🎉

Anyone who visits your link can create an account and start journaling.

## To charge money for Plus/Group plans

The easiest way (no coding needed):
1. Go to **gumroad.com** and create a free seller account
2. Create two products: "Dear Diary Plus - $6/month" and "Dear Diary Group - $12/month"
3. After someone pays, you go into their account manually and upgrade their plan
   - In the app, plans can be upgraded on the Pricing tab by clicking "Upgrade"
   - (Future: this can be automated with Stripe — ask Claude to add that!)
